Banana Bread


This is a nutty chocolatey banana bread recipe. Horaay.

This is sort of the recipe I follow when making my own banana bread.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 very ripe bananas, peeled and mashed
  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 honey
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons whole fat greek yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • toasted walnuts, to taste
  • mini semi-sweet chocolate chips, to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F degrees
  2. In a medium bowl, sift together flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt
  3. In a large bowl, beat sugar, eggs, honey, and oil until light and fluffy. Add in mashed bananas, vanilla, and greek yogurt.
  4. Slowly fold in dry ingredients, nuts, and chocolate chips
  5. Pour batter into a greased or buttered loaf pan, crack flakes of sea salt over top
  6. Bake for about 45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into center of bread comes out clean.
  7. Remove from oven, and let cool/ Give into temptation and nibble at golden edges.
