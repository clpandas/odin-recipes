Cacio e pepe!


This is a simple (but maybe not so) classic cacio e pepe recipe!

Perfect for a simple night in :)

Ingredients

  • Bucatini or spaghetti noodles
  • Pecorino romano
  • Black pepper
  • Reserved pasta water
  • Optional: butter

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of water to boil and generously season with salt.
  2. Add pasta and cook until al dente.
  3. Drian pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  4. Place skillet over medium heat and add pasta.
  5. Incrementally add reserved pasta water (and butter).
  6. Stir/toss together.
  7. Add grated cheese and black pepper and continue to toss until sauce comes together and is smooth and creamy.
